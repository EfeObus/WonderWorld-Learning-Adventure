# Privacy Policy for WonderWorld Learning Adventure

**Last Updated:** January 1, 2026

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

---

## Introduction

WonderWorld Learning Adventure ("the App", "we", "our", "us") is a children's educational application designed for children ages 2-8. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and families who use our application.

This privacy policy explains how the App handles information in compliance with:
- **COPPA** (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)
- **GDPR-K** (General Data Protection Regulation - Children's provisions)
- **Google Play Families Policy**

---

## Information We Collect

### We Do NOT Collect:
- ❌ Personal information (names, emails, phone numbers)
- ❌ Account credentials or passwords
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Photos or videos
- ❌ Contact lists
- ❌ Advertising identifiers
- ❌ Payment information
- ❌ Any data transmitted to external servers

### We DO Store Locally:
- ✅ Learning progress (stars earned, activities completed)
- ✅ Sound and music preferences
- ✅ Letters and words practiced
- ✅ Game scores and achievements

**All data is stored ONLY on the device** and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

---

## How We Use Information

The locally stored progress data is used solely to:
- Track educational achievements within the app
- Resume progress when returning to activities
- Display motivational feedback (stars, streaks)

---

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally on the device using encrypted storage
- Data is not backed up to cloud services
- Data is not shared with any third parties
- Data is not used for advertising or analytics

---

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following device features:
- **Microphone**: For voice recognition during learning activities (speech-to-text). Voice data is processed by the device's built-in speech recognition service (Google Speech Services on Android, Siri on iOS) and is subject to those services' privacy policies. We do not store or transmit voice recordings.
- **Speaker**: For reading content aloud using the device's text-to-speech engine
- **Internet**: Required only for voice recognition processing by the device's speech service. No user data is transmitted to our servers.

We do NOT integrate:
- Advertising SDKs
- Analytics services
- Social media sharing
- In-app purchases

---

## Parental Controls

Parents can:
- Delete all stored progress data by clearing app data in device settings
- Uninstall the app to remove all associated data
- Control microphone access through device permissions
- Disable voice recognition features by denying microphone permission

---

## Children's Privacy

This App is designed specifically for children and:
- Does not require account creation
- Does not collect personal information
- Does not include social features
- Does not contain advertisements
- Does not allow in-app purchases
- Does not link to external websites
- Does not allow communication with other users

---

## Data Retention

Progress data is stored on the device indefinitely until:
- The app is uninstalled
- App data is cleared from device settings
- The device is reset

We do not retain any data on external servers as we do not collect or transmit user data.

---

## International Users

This app is available globally. All data processing occurs locally on your device regardless of your location. No data crosses international borders as we do not transmit data to external servers.

---

## Your Rights

As a user or parent/guardian, you have the right to:
- **Access**: View progress data within the app
- **Delete**: Remove all data by clearing app data or uninstalling
- **Restrict**: Disable microphone permissions to prevent voice features
- **Object**: Choose not to use certain features

Since all data is stored locally and we have no access to it, you have complete control over your data.
